# Dietary Context

*Current eating patterns and dietary framework.*

## Core Dietary Identity

### Vegan
Plant-based diet. No animal products.

### Sugar Minimization
Minimises direct sugar intake. This means avoiding added sugars and high-sugar foods, not necessarily avoiding all naturally occurring sugars in whole foods.

### Time-Restricted Eating
**Eating window**: 12:00 pm – 7:30 pm

An intermittent fasting pattern with approximately a 7.5-hour eating window and a 16.5-hour fasting period. First meal is lunch; no eating after early evening.

## Current Patterns

### Two Eating Contexts

**Home (Somerset)**
- Household meals are vegan for me; household member may add fish to their portion

*Typical Home Lunch:*
- Heavily bread-based — bread is ~80% brown sourdough
- At least 2 pieces of toast with avocado (heavily spread), salt, pepper, sometimes spice-infused oil

- 2× daily: 300ml fortified soy milk + pea protein + 1 tbsp chia seeds

*Cavolo Nero:*

*Chia Pudding Option:*

*Evening snack — REDUCED:*
- Previously: berries + plant milk + granola + chocolate

## Hydration & Caffeine

### Morning Routine (Pre-Eating Window)
- **"Breakfast"**: Glass of water before first coffee
- **First coffee**: 7:45–9:30 am — strong, large

### During Eating Window
- **Second coffee**: 12:00–2:30 pm
- **Third coffee**: Rare (~once a month), usually social context in a café

### Tea
Almost always has a cup of tea on the go:
- **Caffeinated (Earl Grey)**: Up to 2 cups, between 3:30–6:30 pm
- **Herbal**: Camomile or similar — no time restriction

### Turmeric-Ginger Tea (4 days/week)
Homemade preparation:
- Stem ginger + turmeric boiled in a pan
- Black pepper added for curcumin absorption (~2000% increase)
- Anti-inflammatory, whole-food approach (not curcumin supplements)
- Note: mild blood-thinning effect — be aware given nattokinase use; mention both before any surgery

## Calcium Strategy (Food-Based)

**Target**: ~1000-1200mg/day
**Approach**: All food-based (K2 from MultiVit directs calcium to bones)

### Daily Sources

| Source | Calcium | Notes |
|--------|---------|-------|
| 2× soy milk shakes | ~720mg | Own-brand unsweetened (must be fortified) |
| 2× chia seeds (1 tbsp each) | ~190mg | In shakes; 15-20 min hydration |
| Cavolo nero | ~100mg | From garden; steamed, with meals |
| Hummus + chickpeas | ~125mg | Generous daily intake |
| Tofu (2×/week) | ~90mg | Calcium-set |
| Lentils | ~35mg | Adding daily |
| **Total** | **~1260mg** | |

### Key Decisions

- **Soy milk**: Own-brand unsweetened — same calcium as Alpro at half the price
- **Greens**: Cavolo nero preferred (easier prep than curly kale); NOT spinach (oxalates block absorption)
- **No blender in London**: Shaker bottle sufficient for soy milk + protein + chia
- **Chia**: Hydrate 15-20 mins in shake, or make chia pudding dessert
